Taoism

There once was a mysterious man that lived in ancient China. Born and raised in the state of Chu (currently called as Henan), this obscure figure worked in the great halls the powerful Zhou Dynasty in around 600BCE. He served in the court as a Shi. He was a scholar specialized in things like astrology and divination and were in charge of sacred books.
